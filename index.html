Our Story

  • Haytam Parfumerie Opened

    We took a step in the world of beauty and care products with the establishment of Haytam Parfumerie. It marked the beginning of a vision to bring exquisite products to our clientele.

    2017

  • Website Launch

    We embraced the digital frontier with the launch of our e-commerce platform, HaytamParfumerie.com. This move not only expanded our reach but also provided a seamless shopping experience for our Clientele across the country during the COVID19 Pandemic.

    2019

  • Wholesale Network Establishment

    Our horizons broadened as we ventured into wholesaling and reselling, extending the allure of our products to perfumeries across the country. This was a pivotal moment as we became not just a retailer but a key player in the distribution landscape.

    2021

  • Bendemrane Distribution Founded

    The launch of Bendemrane Distribution marked a strategic shift in our operations. This move allowed us to efficiently navigate a network that expanded from perfumeries to include parapharmacies, Drug Stores, other wholesalers, and thriving e-commerce businesses, fostering growth and reliability.

    2022

  • HairCare Proteins Introduction

    We proudly unveiled our very own hair protein brand, HairCare Brazil Proteins. This marks a significant milestone in our journey, as we step into the realm of hair care, blending quality and innovation to provide our customers with the best in hair nourishment.

    2023

  • Introduction of Three New American brands

    We undertook a significant stride by introducing the Moroccan market to three distinguished American brands: Aztec Secret, Yeouth Skincare, and J Beverly Hills. Broadening our product portfolio and also bringing forth innovative and acclaimed offerings in skincare and haircare to The Moroccan Consumer.
